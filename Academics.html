Overview

Each academic year consists of two terms. The first term is from June to November while the second term is from December to April. There will be Winter Vacation between the terms of nearly fifteen days. The Summer Vacation will be from April to June.

Classes

The Class timings are as follows :-
Mini.KG to Sr.KG : Monday to Friday - 8:35 AM to 12:00 PM
I std to VIII std : Monday to Friday - 8:35 AM to 2:00 PM
IX std to X std : Monday to Friday - 8:35 AM to 3:00 PM
Common for all : Saturday - 8:35 AM to 12:00 PM (Half Day)
Sunday - Holiday

Examinations

In total, there will be six exams in an year, four unit tests(monthly) and two term exams(half yearly) equally divided among the two terms. Only the final term exam will be considered for the evaluation of the year's grade. Yet, all exams are equally important and students who fail in any exam must appear for supplementary exams. Even if most of the exams do not carry grade, they are essential for constant monitoring of a student's growth.
