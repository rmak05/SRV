Overview

Samarth Ramdas Vidyalaya was established by Cenka Samaj Kalyan Sanstha in 1996 in Mahad, Raigad Distict, Maharashtra. The campus is spread across 3000 square metres in the lush green region on the banks of Savitri River. The School is two floors high with classes right from Mini.KG till 10th standard.

Sports Facilities

The school boasts two playing grounds. One ground is small and is within the campus while other is a cricket ground just outside the campus. During PT period, students will taken to the grounds to indulge them in physical activities ensured that they are under guidance of PT teachers. Equipment for Cricket, Badminton, Volleyball, Football, Chess, Lagori are available to use during PT period only.

Lab Facilities

The school has a science lab which is a one-stop destination for all physical, chemical and biological experiments. The lab has all the necessary chemicals and apparatus for chemistry related experiments. The lab has physical instruments like vernier calipers, screw gauge, lenses for physics related experiments. The lab has preserved specimens of various organisms for observation and analysis. The lab also has a compound microscope.

E-Learning

All classes from 5th to 10th standard have projector and screen installed. Teachers can opt to teach using digital classes with animation and sound. This will lead in a better learning experience for the student and classes will be less broing and more productive.
